Meet the Sinclair Dinosaur

A life-size fiberglass Corythosaurus dinosaur from the Sinclair Dinoland exhibit at the 1964-65 New York World's Fair It is one of nine life-size dinosaurs designed by Louis Paul Jonas for Sinclair. After the fair, these models toured the U.S. before being placed in various locations, with Independence receiving this particular model.
